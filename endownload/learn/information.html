Important Information

Drivers Licence:

You must have a current Licence and/or an international licence
The minimum age for hiring a rental car is generally 21

Impaired Driving:

Drugs and/or medication which can effect your driving is illegal
There is a zero alcohol limit for drivers under 20;
Drivers over 20 are advised to consumer no more than two standard drinks before driving

Seatbelts:

You must wear seatbelts, failing to do so can incur a $150 fine

Cellphones:

You must not use a mobile phone while driving unless it is completely hands free
or mounted to the vehicle and touched infrequently.

Crashes:

If you are involved in a crash you must stop and call the police (111) if someone is injured.
Obtain vehicle registration numbers and notify the insurance company and/or rental car company within 24 hours.
If no one is hurt give your name and address to the property owner or the police if the owner cant be located.
