 Notifications

SafeDriver provides regular alerts to drivers to notify them when driving around dangerous areas and to remind them of some of the critical road rules

If you prefer to not receive these notifications press the back button on your device, press settings and turn of the alerts you wish.

It is recommended to not turn off the location based alerts as they only sound when on dangerous roads and they provide essential information and messages.
