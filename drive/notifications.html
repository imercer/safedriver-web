 Notifications

SafeDriver provides regular alerts to drivers to notify them when driving around dangerous areas and to remind them of some of the critical road rules

If you prefer to not receive these notifications please visit your Device Settings, open up SafeDriver and turn off location access to stop receiving location based alerts and turn off notifications to turn all messages off. If you want to pause them for a while you can turn on Do Not Disturb mode.
